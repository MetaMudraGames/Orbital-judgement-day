# Code of Conduct

---

## Our Commitment

ASPHYXIA creates experiences that challenge players intellectually. Our community should challenge contributors constructively.

We are committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, nationality
- Level of experience, education, socioeconomic status
- Personal appearance, race, religion, sexual orientation

---

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

```
╔═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                                                                 ║
║   RESPECT                                                       ║
║   Treat others as you would want to be treated.                 ║
║   Disagree with ideas, not people.                              ║
║                                                                 ║
║   CONSTRUCTIVENESS                                              ║
║   Criticism should aim to improve.                              ║
║   Offer solutions, not just complaints.                         ║
║                                                                 ║
║   PATIENCE                                                      ║
║   Review takes time. People have lives.                         ║
║   Assume good intent until proven otherwise.                    ║
║                                                                 ║
║   ACCOUNTABILITY                                                ║
║   Own your mistakes. Learn from them.                           ║
║   Accept feedback gracefully.                                   ║
║                                                                 ║
║   SAFETY FIRST                                                  ║
║   Player wellbeing is non-negotiable.                           ║
║   If you see concerning content, report it.                     ║
║                                                                 ║
╚═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Unacceptable Behavior

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                                 │
│   ▪ Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination                 │
│   ▪ Personal attacks or insults                                 │
│   ▪ Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments              │
│   ▪ Publishing others' private information                      │
│   ▪ Sexual content or unwelcome advances                        │
│   ▪ Sustained disruption of discussions                         │
│   ▪ Advocating for any of the above                             │
│                                                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Repository interactions (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Official communication channels
- Representing the project publicly
- Community events or spaces

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

**Email:** MetaMudraGames@gmail.com

All reports will be:
- Reviewed promptly
- Kept confidential
- Investigated fairly

### Consequences

```
LEVEL 1: WARNING
First minor violation. Private correction.

LEVEL 2: TEMPORARY BAN
Repeated or serious violations. 
Temporary exclusion from community spaces.

LEVEL 3: PERMANENT BAN
Severe violations or pattern of behavior.
Permanent exclusion from all project spaces.
```

Decisions are made by project maintainers and are final.

---

## Philosophy

ASPHYXIA games are demanding. They don't hold players' hands. They let failure happen.

But our community is not a game.

Here, we support each other. We build together. We make challenging experiences accessible to those who seek them.
