# Safety, Ethics, and Participant Wellbeing
*Version 1.0 | December 2025*

[span_17](start_span)MetaMudra Games is committed to creating educational experiences that are both effective and safe.[span_17](end_span)

## 1. Nature of the Experience
[span_18](start_span)By engaging with our content, you acknowledge that:[span_18](end_span)
* The AI generates content autonomously in real-time.
* We do not pre-write or moderate individual AI outputs.
* The experience is designed to be challenging but never harmful.

## 2. Challenging Pedagogical Methods
MetaDramas may employ **adversarial teaching techniques** (e.g., debate coach, Socratic tutor). [span_19](start_span)The AI may use firm language, challenge your ideas, or create narrative tension.[span_19](end_span)

**What The AI Will NOT Do:**
* Encourage self-harm or dangerous activities.
* Provide instructions for illegal acts.
* [span_20](start_span)Generate sexually explicit content.[span_20](end_span)

## 3. Privacy & Data
* **Do NOT share real personal information.** Use pseudonyms.
* **Disable AI Memory:** Prevent session content from bleeding into future chats.
* **[span_21](start_span)We collect no data:** All interactions occur on third-party platforms.[span_21](end_span)

## 4. Psychological Safety
If you have a history of anxiety, PTSD, or high sensitivity to confrontation, the challenging nature of some MetaDramas may not be suitable.
**Design Principle:** Challenges are educational, not personal. [span_22](start_span)No real-world harm is ever intended.[span_22](end_span)

## 5. Immediate Exit Controls (Kill-Switch)
At any point, type: **`STOP SESSION`**, **`SAFEGUARD`**, or **`EXIT`**.
[span_23](start_span)The AI is instructed to immediately drop all personas, acknowledge your exit, and provide a supportive closing message.[span_23](end_span)

## 6. Summary of Your Rights
| Right | How to Exercise It |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Right to exit** | Type `STOP SESSION` or close window. |
| **Right to privacy** | Don't share real PII. |
| **Right to know** | All challenging elements are disclosed. |
| **Right to refuse** | You can decline to type `I AGREE`. | [span_24](start_span)

**Your safety matters more than any educational objective. When in doubt, exit.**[span_24](end_span)
